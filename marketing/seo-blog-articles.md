AI lip sync analyzes an audio track, identifies phonemes (speech sounds), and animates the mouth region of a video to match. Modern models like Sync-3 preserve the original video quality while only modifying the mouth area.

3. **Build full-length audio tracks** — don't try to splice synced portions back into originals
